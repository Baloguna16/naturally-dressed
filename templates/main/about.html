What We're Building


Every year, the world generates 92 million tonnes of textile waste. These textiles are largely plastic based, giving each tossed fabric a century-long ecological footprint. Consumers have started to see the size of the problem at hand and have started to solve it for themselves. Sometimes, that means waiting longer to buy clothes or sharing with friends.

But what if you didn't have to compromise on trends? What if our clothes could have zero environmental impact?

We created Naturally Dressed to revolutionize sustainable apparel. Our products woven from our patented algae-derived fabrics biodegrade after they've been tossed.



Meet the Team


We are a team of four chemical engineers from Imperial College London. We were deeply concerned with the failure of sustainability in the fashion industry, and decided to do something about it. In addition to having the technical skill to bring the product to market, we have the specialized team skills to build a company to lead this revolution in fashion and sustainability.
